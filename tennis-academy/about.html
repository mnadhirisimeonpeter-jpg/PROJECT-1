Our Story

About Ace Tennis Academy

Where passion for tennis meets professional development

Our Mission

More Than a Sport. A Standard.

Ace Tennis Academy was founded in 2018 with one purpose: to give Nairobi's athletes a structured, professional environment where they could grow — not just as players, but as disciplined, competitive individuals.

We operate at the Sport Complex, running Monday and Wednesday fitness sessions and daily court training. Our programme is built around three pillars: technique, fitness, and mental resilience.

Our Core Values

Discipline

Consistent attendance, punctuality, and effort. We build habits that extend beyond the tennis court into every area of life.

Development

Every player follows a personalised development plan with measurable milestones. We track progress, not just participation.

Community

A respectful, inclusive environment where players support each other's growth. Competition drives us; community sustains us.
