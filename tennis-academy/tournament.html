Ace Open Tournament 2026

32 players. Group stage + knockout. One champion.

Event Overview

About the Tournament

The Ace Open is our annual flagship tournament — two days of competitive tennis open to members and invited players from across Nairobi. The event combines a group stage for everyone to get court time with a knockout phase that determines the overall champion.

All matches are played at the Sport Complex. The tournament is organized and supervised by Ace Tennis Academy coaching staff.

📅 Dates

28–29 June 2026

📍 Venue

Sport Complex, Nairobi

👥 Players

32+ registered

🎾 Format

Groups + Knockout

Tournament Format

Day 1 — Group Stage

Players are divided into 8 groups of 4. Each player faces every other player in their group. Top 2 from each group advance to the knockout rounds.

Day 2 — Knockout Stage

Quarter-finals (16 players) → Semi-finals (8) → Final (2). 3rd place playoff included. Prize-giving at 5:00 PM.
